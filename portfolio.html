Project 3: The Viral Effect
Click here to view app
Click here for GitHub respository
Description

The Viral Effect tracks the impact of the Corona Virus on the US. Users can signup and log in to view how the Corona Virus has impacted the closing price of the Consumer Descretionary fund ETF over the time since the first case in the world was diagnosed, and the number of cases in the world over the same period. There is a comment section for users to enter comments and have a conversation on what they are seeing. On the industries page users can view the pre-covid-19 information for different industries based on how the Bureau of Labor defines industries and then enter in an assumption of how Covid-19 will impact the industry and see the impact on wages and what the hit to economy may be.
The data for the industries comes from the BLS, the stock data comes from WorldTradingData, and the Corona Virus data comes from Johns Hopkins.

Technologies used: React, Bootstrap-for-React, JavaScript, Data Scraping, API, express, axios, chart.js, Sequelize

Budget Tracker App
Click here to view app
Click here for GitHub repository
Description

The Budget Tracker App allows users to track their incoming and outgoing payments. Users are able to input the name of a transaction, the amount, and whether they are adding funds or subtracting funds. Users can then see a list of their transactions as well as a graph showing their total over time. History is tracked in a database, and the app can be used offline, and any transactions made offline will be stored to be processed once a user goes online. The app is also designed to be a Progressive Web App (PWA) and is mobile friendly.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MongoDB, IndexedDB, PWA
